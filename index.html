Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform and services

How does the matchmaking process work?

Our matchmaking process uses a proprietary algorithm that analyzes your business needs, industry, size, and goals to identify compatible partners. We combine AI technology with human expertise to ensure quality matches.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients begin seeing potential matches within the first week of using our platform. The timeline for establishing partnerships varies based on your industry and specific requirements, but we typically facilitate initial connections within 2-3 weeks.

Is my data secure with your platform?

Yes, we take data security extremely seriously. Our platform uses enterprise-grade encryption, regular security audits, and compliance with industry standards including GDPR and CCPA. We never share your confidential information without explicit permission.

Can I integrate with my existing CRM?

Yes, we offer integrations with popular CRM platforms including Salesforce, HubSpot, and Microsoft Dynamics. Custom integrations are also available for enterprise clients with proprietary systems.

What industries do you serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including technology, manufacturing, healthcare, financial services, retail, and professional services. Our platform is designed to be adaptable to various business models and partnership types.
